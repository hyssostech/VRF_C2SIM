## Hypothesis / design (full rationale in the design note)
F3 (COA-STP1 scale run, sec 4c) was TWO problems from straggler==predecessor==600 s:
  (i)  THE RACE: straggler synthesis tied the successors' predecessor windows; skip won
       every time -> straggler completions unblocked NO successors (15 skip-gated).
  (ii) UNDERSIZED: 600 s < real march time for 13-40 km routes at 20x -> straggler fired
       mid-march (premature) instead of letting the unit complete via real quorum.
Fix tested (ONE variable family vs the scale run): raise the straggler ABOVE real march
times AND keep it below the predecessor timeout.
  Vrf__FanOutStragglerSeconds        600 -> 900
  Vrf__TaskPredecessorTimeoutSeconds 600 -> 1200
All else IDENTICAL to the scale run: ClientId=C2SIM, DeStackCreates=true,
AggregateFormation=auto, SubordinateFanOut=true, TimeMultiplier=20,
